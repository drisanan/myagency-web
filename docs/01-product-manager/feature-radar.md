Summary
Radar surfaces athlete discovery feeds, trends, and tracking. Mirrors mobile Radar and RadarSocials experiences.

Users
- Recruiter, Coach, Athlete

User Stories
- As a recruiter/coach, I can browse trending athletes and follow or add to a watchlist.
- As a user, I can view social metrics (followers, growth) and see changes over time.
- As a user, I can filter radar by sport, tags, or social platform.

Acceptance Criteria
- Feeds are paginated and cached client-side.
- Social metrics and deltas match mobile calculations.
- Watchlist persists to services with tenant isolation.
- Loading/empty/error states covered by tests.

KPIs
- Engagement with radar feeds (views, follows).
- Watchlist growth and revisit rates.
