WHY Athlete Narrative Matters

The college recruiting process is stressful and broken for most families. Unless your athlete is in the top one percent, it often feels impossible to get noticed. Athlete Narrative was created by two dads who lived this problem and decided to fix it. With our app, you can simplify recruiting into one easy system that saves time, reduces stress, and gives your athlete a real chance to be seen. Download it now in the Apple Store or Google Play Store and start your journey today.

Built for Athletes First

We designed Athlete Narrative with the athlete at the center. Profiles are quick to build, highlight reels are easy to upload, and every feature feels as natural to use as TikTok or Instagram. Athletes can research any school in the country, compare their options, and send personalized emails to coaches with the tap of a button. This is the fastest way for student athletes to take control of their recruiting story.

The Parent Advantage

Parents want to help, but the line between support and stress can feel thin. Athlete Narrative was built to give parents peace of mind. Weekly updates keep you in the loop without micromanaging. You will know exactly where your child stands in the process, which schools they are targeting, and how coaches are responding. With the system built by two dads who have been there, you can finally support your child with confidence instead of worry.
